BORN

My story began on January 5th, 2007, in Ivano-Frankivsk, Ukraine. From a young age, I loved to travel and explore the world. Like all children, I wanted to grow up quickly. I always tried to do everything on time, manage resources, and never be late. This helped me become who I am today and acquire truly important Soft Skills.

SCHOOL

I excelled in school and frequently participated in mathematics and physics Olympiads and competitions, often achieving prize-winning positions. I thoroughly enjoyed working in teams, and such contests were often organized at my school. In high school, I had a circle of friends who shared my passion for programming and computers. We frequently embarked on various projects together. It was there that I also tried myself in a leadership role, assigning tasks, assessing what would work best for each person, and ensuring everything went according to plan.

COMPUTER SCHOOL

I started attending a programming school when I was 12 years old. It was there, at that time, that my fascination and interest in programming emerged. I often studied ahead, as I enjoyed creating something new. The education in this school lasted for four years. During this time, I managed to acquire programming skills, learn a great deal about IT, develop the ability to learn independently, and enhance skills such as self-discipline, time management, and teamwork.

FIRST WORK EXPERIENCE

I received my first work experience at the Ivano-Frankivsk National Medical University. In 2022, when I was 15 years old, I was hired as a web developer. My task was to create a platform for students that would help them gain knowledge without leaving their homes. It was "EducationHub". Due to the situation in the country, this project became very useful and necessary. The presentation took place in November of the same year.
